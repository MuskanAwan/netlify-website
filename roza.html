RAMADAN K ROZY KI AHMIYAT
Spiritual Cleansing: Fasting during Ramadan is considered a form of spiritual purification. It helps Muslims cleanse their souls, seek forgiveness, and develop self-discipline. Reflection and Gratitude: It provides an opportunity for Muslims to reflect on their actions, increase their devotion to Allah, and express gratitude for the blessings in their lives. Community Bonding: Ramadan fosters a sense of community and solidarity among Muslims worldwide. Through shared fasting and communal prayers, individuals strengthen their bonds with family, friends, and the larger Muslim community.










Ramzan Ka Roza Quran-E-Kareem Ki Roshni Main


“ALLAH Rabbul Aalameen Quran-E-Kareem Me Irshad Farmata Hai” يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ كُتِبَ عَلَيْكُمُ ٱلصِّيَامُ كَمَا كُتِبَ عَلَى ٱلَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَتَّقُونَ أَيَّامًا مَّعْدُودَٰتٍ ۚ فَمَن كَانَ مِنكُم مَّرِيضًا أَوْ عَلَىٰ سَفَرٍ فَعِدَّةٌ مِّنْ أَيَّامٍ أُخَرَ ۚ وَعَلَى ٱلَّذِينَ يُطِيقُونَهُۥ فِدْيَةٌ طَعَامُ مِسْكِينٍ ۖ فَمَن تَطَوَّعَ خَيْرًا فَهُوَ خَيْرٌ لَّهُۥ ۚ وَأَن تَصُومُوا۟ خَيْرٌ لَّكُمْ ۖ إِن كُنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ….. Aye imanwalo Tum Par Roza Farz Kiya Gaya.. Jaisa Unpe Farz Hua Tha Jo Tum Se Pehle Huye.. Taki Tum Gunaho Se Bacho, Phir Tum Me Jo Bimar Ho Ya Safar Me Ho Wo Aur Dino Me Ginti Puri Kare (Bimari Ya Safar Khatm Hone Ke Bad Roza Rakhe), Aur Jo TAQAT Nahi Rakhte (Boodha Aadmi Ya Aisa Bimar Jiske Tandurust Hone Ki Ummid Na Ho) Wo Fidya Den, Eik Miskeen Ka Khana Phir Jo Ziyadah Bhalai Kare Ye Uske Liye Behtar Hai, Aur Roza Rakhna Tumhare Liye Behtar Hai Agar Tum Jante Ho {قرآن كريم، پارہ-2، سورة البقرة-187, ترجمہ، کنزالایمان، از-اعلی حضرت علیہ الرحمہ} Ramzan Ka Mahina Jisme Quran Utra. Logo Ke Liye Hidayat Aur Rehnumai Aur Faisle Ki Roshan Bate To Tum Me Jo Koi Ye Mahina Paye Jaroor Iske Roze Rakhe. Aur Jo Bimar Hai Ya Safar Me Hai To Itne Roze Aur Dino Me, ALLAH Tum Par Aasani Chahta Hai Aur Tum Par Dushwari Nahi Chahta Aur Isliye Ki Tum Ginti Puri Karo. Aur ALLAH Ki Badhai Bolo Is Par Ki Usne Tumhe Hidayat Ki Aur Kahi Tum Haq Guzar Ho. (Sure Bakrah, Para 2, Aayat 185, Tarzuma: Kanzul Imaan)





Rozy ki Fazilat Hadees ki Roshni mein:


Mahfum-e-Hadees:


  1. Aadmi Ke Nek Kaam Ka Badla 10 Se 700 Tak Diya Jata Hai... Allah Tala Ne Farmaya Magar Roza Mere Liye Hai Aur Mai Hi uski Jaza Dunga... Banda apni Khwahish Aur Khane Ko Meri Wajah Se Chhod Deta Hai... Rozadar Ke Liye 2 Khushiyan Hain Eik iftar Ke WAQT Aur Eik Apne Rab Se Milne Ke WAQT Aur Rozadar Ke Muh Ki Boo Allah Ke Nazdeek Mushk Se Pakizah Hai... Aur Roza Dhaal Hai (admi ke liye Bachao ka Hatiyar) Aur Jab Koi Roze Se Ho To Be-hudah Na Bake Na Chillaye... aur jab Koi Rozadar Se Gali (Gaali Galoch) Kare Ya Ladne Ko Aaye To Kahe Mai Roze Se Hun. {مشكاة المصابيح، كتاب الصوم، الفصل الأول، جلد-1، صفحہ-541، الحديث-1959}

  2. – Hadees-e Mafhoom: Jisme Allah ke Rasool (Sallallahu Alaihi Wasallam) Ne Irshad Farmaya - Roza Dhaal hai. Jab tumme se koi Roze Se Ho to Behayai Ki Baten Na Kare or Shor na kare.Agar koi Use Gaali de ya usse Ladai Jhagda kare to kehde ME ROZE DAR HU. – (BUKHARI)
